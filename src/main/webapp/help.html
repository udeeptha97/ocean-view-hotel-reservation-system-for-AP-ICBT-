How to use the Room Reservation System.

Login

Open login.html, enter your username/password, and click Login.

Add Reservation

Go to Add Reservation, fill the form, and click Save.

Display Reservation

Enter a reservation number to view details.

Calculate & Print Bill

Enter reservation number, generate bill, and print.

This is UI-only. When you build Java Servlets + JDBC, these pages will send forms to your servlet URLs.
